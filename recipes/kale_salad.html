Kale Salad

Description

I found myself making this salad a lot during the pandemic. I love beets and eggs in salad, and I think a lot people can agree that roasted or grilled chicken on a salad is delightful.

I buy the Sweet Kale salad from Costco for this. You can probably make your own kale salad from scratch, but part of the reason for this is I'm not really a great cook, and I still wanted to eat relatively healthily. I also buy the prepared beets from Costco because roasting my own was always a pain.

Ingredients

  • Sweet Kale bagged salad
  • Hard boiled eggs (4-6)
  • Roasted or grilled chicken
  • Cooked beets

Steps

  1. Dump salad without dressing into a large salad bowl
  2. Chop chicken, beets, and eggs to desired size and add to salad bowl
  3. Add the rest of the salad stuff (dressings and other little toppings)
